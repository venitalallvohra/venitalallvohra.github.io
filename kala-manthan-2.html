Exhibitions

Kala Manthan-2

Canvas Art Gallery commemorated its inaugural anniversary with a remarkable celebration, extending a warm invitation to talented artists to exhibit their masterpieces within its elegant confines. The prestigious exhibition, "Kala Manthan-2," was expertly curated by artist Bhanu Govani, featuring a collective of 15 distinguished artists from India. It was a source of immense joy for me to be a part of this artistic showcase. The Guest of Honour for this auspicious occasion was none other than Ar. Manoj Kumar. The exhibition made its grand debut on the 13th of March, gracing the gallery with its creative brilliance for a month, concluding on the 13th of April 2023.
